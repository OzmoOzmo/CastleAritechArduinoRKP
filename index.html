CastleAritechArduinoRKP

An Aritech Alarm compatible Remote Keypad that makes Alarm functions accessible via any desktop or modern mobile phone browser.

Allows you to remote arm/disarm the panel as well as view logs etc. Also Emails you when an Alarm happens.

Connection to the Panel requires either only a two or a four wire connection (your choice).

To compile, place all in a folder called "CastleAritechArduinoRKP" and open the "CastleAritechArduinoRKP.ino" in Arduino IDE.

No libraries are required.

The circuit required is in the HowTo Notes Folder.
